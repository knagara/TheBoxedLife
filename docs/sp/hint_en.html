Stage 1 "Getting lost in the labyrinth"

Touch the man and drag him to EXIT.

Stage 2 "I'm alone in my room"

Stage 3 "Being puzzled in neon signs"

Goal is not always on right hand side.

To jump farther, tap arrow key twice during jump.
Still more, move before jumping.

To stop faster, tap the other side arrow key after jumping.

You cannot tap jump and arrow buttons at the same time.

When you enter TEPCO, generate an earthquake by shaking.

Stage 25 "Other people always hurt my heart"

Sorry, this stage can be solved only by Japanese speakers.
Stop at 992.

This is like a word puzzle of Japanese language.
9 = nine = "kyu" or "ku"
2 = two = "tsu" or "ni"

"The Boxed Life" -> "Kyukutsu na Jinsei"（窮屈な人生）
Kyukutsu -> Kyu ku tsu -> 9 9 2
